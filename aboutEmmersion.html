Are your kids future ready?

E-mmersion.ca is a tutoring company dedicated to teaching digital literacy to school-aged students; helping them prepare for life.

Digital lieteracy is rapidly becoming a hot topic in most educational spheres, and for good reason. Children who learn these skills earlier in life tend to learn them more effectively, and are able to use them more consistently in the world around them.

What are the benefits?

Coding and programming is one of the most sought after skillsets in today’s job market. At Emmersion.ca, I help students explore web design and JavaScript programming through a series of courses called: ‘Coding like Cursive’. The courses are eight weeks long, cover three computer languages, and teach students the fundamentals of how to build a business in the process.

In addition to courses on building websites and JavaScript programming, I also offer courses on working with spreadsheets, and writing for a business audience, two technical skills that are increasingly in-demand in the world of digital marketing and big data extraction. These courses can be taken on their own, or alongside the popular 'Coding like Cursive' courses, for the full, business immersion experience.

About the Tutor

Ryan holds a Master of Education degree from the University of Calgary, specializing in 'Teaching and Learning Languages' and 'Designing Technology-rich, Collaborative Learning Environments'. He coaches literacy and personal development for all ages, specializing in digital literacy, web development, entrepreneurship, and business fundamentals.
