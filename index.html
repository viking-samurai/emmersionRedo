Coding Lessons

Emmersion.ca is a tutoring company that specializes in teaching literacy and technology to school-aged students. At Emmersion, I offer courses in JavaScript programming and website development, specifically designed for school-aged students and their families. Coding and programming is now one of the most sought after skillsets in North America, especially as automation moves into the workforce. Why not start learning today?

Collaborative Learning

Collaborative learning is a new look at group learning that promotes both teamwork and individual growth, for students looking to learn a similar goal at a similar pace. At Emmersion, all of my courses are offered as either one on one instruction, or as collaborative learning opportunities, with up to four students per class. I also come to you with my own simple setup, wherever that may be, to make it easy for you stay working.

The Growth Mindset

Emmersion.ca is more than just lessons in coding and programming, or how to draft a spreadsheet. I'm an entrepreneur, with experience at launching multiple businesses, as well as some bonafide rescue operations to my name. What I'm really offering through Emmersion is coaching in a way of life that focuses on developing a growth mindset in your children, to help them develop the resilience to succeed!

Coding Like Cursive (Beginner)

Coding like Cursive (Beginner) introduces students to coding and programming. It starts with HTML5 and CSS3, and prepares students for learning JavaScript at the advanced level.

Coding Like Cursive (Advanced)

Coding Like Cursive (Advanced) picks up from the beginner level, and moves students through more complex concepts, while introducing JavaScript, a popular programming language.

Math Magician

Math Magician is designed to enhance math skills by introducing students to the world of big data through spreadsheet technologies, and how they're used in today's markets.

Essay Engineering

Essay Engineering is a writing course for developing voice and thoroughness of communication. The course looks at writing for a purpose to enhance student ability.
